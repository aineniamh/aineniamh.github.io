Hyde hotel policy

A security deposit of €25 per person, up to a maximum of €400 will be required for all group bookings of 3 rooms or more. Please note this is in addition to the bedroom costs. This security deposit is also required 10 days prior to arrival along with full payment for the group.

Please note the security deposit will be retained by the hotel in the event of the following:
  • Any member of your party causing any damages to hotel property or other hotel residents property.
  • Any member of your party causing serious disturbance/noise issues to other residents.
  • Any other issues arising from your party's stay with us which are deemed applicable by the Manager on duty.
  • No alcohol is to be consumed on the premises unless purchased from the hotel. This includes guest rooms and apartments.
  • Display of sexual paraphernalia, including items of fancy dress, is not permitted in any of the public areas including reception, bar, restaurant and lobby.
  • Check out is 11.30am. Late check out up to 2pm can be added for €25 per room. This must be arranged with the reception team.
If no such issues arise, the security deposit will be refunded in full on Monday morning. Please speak to the manager on duty regarding this requirement on your arrival to the hotel.

It is your responsibility as group organiser to inform the other members of your party of our hotel policies above. Our reservations team will contact you 10 days before your check in date to take the full payment for bedrooms/meals/security deposit and to request rooming list.
